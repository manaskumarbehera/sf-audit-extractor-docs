FAQ

Is my data sent to external servers?
No. Data processing stays local in your browser.

Why is the extension not connecting?
Open a Salesforce tab and refresh the page session.

How do I report bugs?
Use the GitHub issues link with steps and screenshots.

Can I use this in Edge?
Yes, Chromium-based browsers are supported.
