💬 Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install TrackForcePro?
Visit the Chrome Web Store and click "Add to Chrome." The extension is also available for Edge, Firefox, and Opera. See our Quick Start Guide for detailed steps.
Is my Salesforce data secure?
Yes! TrackForcePro processes all data locally in your browser — no data is sent to external servers. The extension only communicates directly with your Salesforce org using your own session credentials. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Which Salesforce editions are supported?
TrackForcePro works with all Salesforce editions: Developer, Professional, Enterprise, and Unlimited. It supports both Production and Sandbox environments.
How do I enable inline field editing?
In the Data Explorer tab, go to All Fields and double-click any field value to edit it. Changes are saved directly to Salesforce via the REST API. You can disable this for Production orgs in Settings > General to prevent accidental changes.
Can I customize my keyboard shortcuts?
Yes! Open the extension and go to Settings > General. You'll see click-to-record buttons for the three main actions: Toggle Sidebar, Toggle Show All Data, and Command Palette. Click any button to rebind it to your preferred keys. Your custom shortcuts are saved automatically.
What if I accidentally edit a field in Production?
You can disable inline editing for Production orgs. Go to Settings > General and enable "Disable Inline Edit in Production." You'll see a warning if you try to edit a field in a Production org when this setting is on. Sandboxes are always editable for testing.
How do I export audit logs?
Navigate to the Audit Trail tab, set your filters (date range, object, action), and click the Export button. You can export in CSV or JSON format for further analysis.
The extension shows "Not Connected" — what do I do?
Make sure you have an active, logged-in Salesforce tab open in your browser. The extension detects your session from open tabs. Log in to Salesforce, keep the tab open, refresh your Salesforce page, and then open the extension again.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Issue Solution
Extension not appearing on Salesforce Ensure you're on a Salesforce page (*.salesforce.com or *.force.com). The TF button appears at the bottom-right corner.
Session expired or "No access token" error Log out and log back into your Salesforce org. Refresh the page. The extension will detect the new session automatically.
Queries not executing or returning errors Verify you have API access enabled in your Salesforce profile. Check object/field names for accuracy. Review the error message for hints.
Objects/fields not loading in builders Check your internet connection. Verify you're logged into Salesforce. Click the Refresh button. Clear your browser cache if issues persist.
Export or download not working Ensure your browser allows downloads from extensions. Check your browser's download settings and security policies.
Inline edit not working Verify the field is editable (not a formula or system field). Check that "Disable Inline Edit in Production" is not blocking you. Ensure you have write permissions on the object.
Platform Events or LMS not streaming Verify the channel exists and you have publish/subscribe permissions. Try refreshing the page. Check for connection errors in the browser console.
