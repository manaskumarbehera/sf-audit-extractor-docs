Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Summary: TrackForcePro does not collect or transmit personal data to external servers. Processing is local to your browser.

Policy quick links

  • Data collection
  • Permissions explained
  • Retention and security

1. Data collection

TrackForcePro does not collect personal data on external infrastructure. No analytics or advertising data pipelines are used.

2. Local processing

  • Salesforce session data is used only for authenticated API actions in your browser context.
  • Preferences are stored locally using browser extension storage APIs.
  • Query results remain local unless you export files yourself.

3. Permissions

  • Tabs/Cookies: detect active Salesforce context and session.
  • Storage: save local settings and cached data.
  • Scripting/Host permissions: enable functionality on Salesforce domains.

4. Retention and deletion

Local data persists until you clear extension storage, reset settings, or uninstall the extension.
