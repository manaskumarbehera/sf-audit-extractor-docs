Privacy Policy

How TrackForcePro handles your data — transparently and securely

Last updated: March 21, 2026
🛡️
Zero Data Collection TrackForcePro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data processing happens locally in your browser.

Introduction

TrackForcePro is a browser extension built to help Salesforce administrators and developers work more efficiently. Whether you're auditing data, building queries, or exploring records, this extension keeps everything under your control—right in your browser.

What We Don't Collect

We don't collect any personal data. Not your queries, not your audit trails, not your Salesforce credentials. The extension never sends information to us or to any external servers. Everything stays on your device, in your browser, under your control.

How Your Data Stays Local

When you use TrackForcePro:

  • Authentication: Your Salesforce session is used only to authenticate with Salesforce's own APIs. We never see your credentials.
  • Query Results: Data you retrieve is displayed in the extension and isn't stored unless you export it yourself.
  • Your Preferences: Settings like theme choice, column widths, and shortcuts are saved locally in your browser, not on our servers.
  • Audit & Explorer Data: All searches, record lookups, and data explorations happen between your browser and Salesforce only.

Browser Permissions Explained

TrackForcePro needs a few permissions to work. Here's what each one does in plain English:

  • Tab Access: Lets us detect when you're on a Salesforce page so we can offer helpful features right where you need them.
  • Cookies: Helps us read your Salesforce session info so you don't have to log in twice. Also serves as a fallback if the service worker restarts.
  • Local Storage: Saves your preferences — theme, keyboard shortcuts, query history, per-org settings, and saved queries — on your device only.
  • Content Scripting: Lets us inject helpful tools directly on Salesforce pages — the Show All Data panel, TF Sidebar, and custom favicons.
  • Context Menus: Enables the right-click menu on Salesforce pages for quick access to Show All Data, Field History, Sharing, SOQL Builder, and Copy Record ID.
  • Browsing Data: Used only for cache management features within the extension. We never clear your browsing data without your action.
  • Salesforce Domains: Allows the extension to communicate with Salesforce APIs (*.salesforce.com, *.force.com, *.lightning.force.com, *.visualforce.com) when you use query builders, records explorer, org tools, and audit features.

Cross-Browser Support

TrackForcePro is currently available on the Chrome Web Store, with Edge and Firefox support coming soon. No matter which browser you use, the same privacy principles apply: your data stays local, no external collection, no tracking. The extension uses Manifest V3 — the latest and most secure extension framework.

Third-Party Services

We don't use any analytics, tracking pixels, advertising networks, or third-party services. The only place TrackForcePro communicates with is Salesforce—the org you're already logged into. That's it.

Data You Control

Your preferences and saved data are yours to manage:

  • Export all settings, query history, and per-org data as JSON (Settings > Backup > Quick Export)
  • Import settings from a backup file to restore or migrate (Settings > Backup > Quick Import)
  • Clear all data directly in the Settings tab
  • Uninstall the extension at any time — all local data is removed automatically
  • Your Salesforce data is never stored unless you explicitly export it
  • Inline editing can be disabled per org for extra safety (Settings > Org Management)

Updates to This Policy

If we make changes to this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of TrackForcePro means you accept any updates.
